## 2. Summary of Sprint Deliverables
Frontend Sprint F2 delivered an exceptional, premium landing page that acts as the entry point for the **AI Placement Platform**. The landing page contains eight modular, data-driven sections that are highly performant and responsive:
1. **Hero Section**: Large stats cards and call-to-actions.
2. **Career Orbit**: Rotating interactive hub with AI Coach center nodes.
3. **Features Deck**: Tabbed mockups of the platform's core dashboard sections.
4. **How It Works**: Step-by-step progress cards connected by visual lines.
5. **Pricing Plans**: Adaptive cost grid highlighting starter and pro categories.
6. **About Section**: Core pain-points cards and mission story text.
7. **Call-to-Action**: Elevated conversion cards on a gradient background.
8. **Landing Footer**: Clean links lists and copyright descriptors.
